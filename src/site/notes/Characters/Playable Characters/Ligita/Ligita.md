## **Backstory:**
---
- Is the embodiment (and a minor deity) of Summer and under the patronage of [[Characters/The Pantheon/Kragnyr\|Kragnyr]]
- Friends with [[Characters/Side Characters/Vetle\|Vetle]], the embodiment of Winter and under the patronage of [[Characters/The Pantheon/Nagaiame\|Nagaiame]]
- Spent most of her existence wandering Tharin and interacting with mortals; was close companions with [[Characters/Playable Characters/Conan Dubhan & Beo/Conan Dubhan & Beo\|Beo]] back when he was an actual living wolf, before he died and got taken into the Lunar Courts
- After Nagaiame's death, Vetle killed themselves, and no one saw it coming because Vetle, as the embodiment of winter, was expected to be gloomy; the others had accepted it as merely a character quirk
- Ligita took it especially poorly because she was so close to him
- Believed [[Characters/The Pantheon/Mizlaba\|Mizlaba]] pushed Vetle to their breaking point in order to take over their role, so she took Vetle's role before Mizlaba could
- Summer took on all Vetle's responsibilities and is completely burning out; totally not meant to exist in the cold, nor to have power over it
- Wants to enact her revenge on Mizlaba for pushing Vetle to suicide
- Joined the AIC ([[Factions/Anti-Icon Coalition\|Anti-Icon Coalition]]), a group located in [[Locations/Tharin/Anadrie/Dunns/Dunns\|Dunns]] dedicated to the destruction and restructure of the pantheon and partnered with the [[Factions/Cult of the Lead Swan\|Cult of the Lead Swan]], to find like-minded individuals who might want to destroy Mizlaba, befriending [[Characters/Playable Characters/Rei/Rei\|Rei]] and [[Characters/Playable Characters/Fabris/Fabris\|Fabris]] while there
- Because of her previous direct experience with the Icons, she is regarded as a highly valuable member and is brought into the "[[Characters/Playable Characters/Unn Oscern/Unn Oscern\|Unn]] Project", a project to turn a boy named Unn into a god so he can wreak havoc on the pantheon
- After learning about the cult's plan to unseal Kragnyr, Ligita is immediately wary, and when [[Characters/Playable Characters/Bolora Dalai/Bolora Dalai\|Bolora Dalai]] destroys the cult headquarters, Ligita uses the chaos to escape the AIC along with Fabris, Rei, Unn, and [[Characters/Playable Characters/Guilloché Maréchal/Guilloché Maréchal\|Guilloché]]; now they are trying to put a stop to the Cult of the Lead Swan's plan to unleash Kragnyr
- Design based around sunflowers; wilting sunflower as a false mane; skull is completely exposed after taking over Winter's domain
## **Etymology:**
---
>"Ligita" comes from the Latvian holiday, Līgo, celebrated at the summer solstice.
