## **Backstory:**
- Taught ancient traditions of divination by his parents
- Owns the [[Locations/Tharin/Muejiza/Galapeak/Auraboros Divination Parlor\|Auraboros Divination Parlor]] in [[Locations/Tharin/Muejiza/Galapeak/Galapeak\|Galapeak]], although he also sells snake oils and other "remedies" 
- Performs divinations to ease people's minds, although he purposely withholds or manipulates what he tells them to do so
- Believes in the importance of hope; would rather people hold misinformed hope and live fully rather than be stuck trying to change the future
- Avoided getting close to people, knowing that they would inevitably grow curious about their future, and he would never have the heart to reject their desire to see
- Was drawn to [[Characters/Playable Characters/Jackdaw/Jackdaw\|Jackdaw]] because Jackdaw is honest about and accepting of his fear of the future, yet they both would rather the unknown so as not to hamper their lives
- At the beginning of their friendship, he agreed to read Jackdaw's omens; stopped immediately once he read that Jackdaw would experience intense suffering
- Has obsessively worried over every possible way Jackdaw's story could end tragically
- Slowly burning inside as he spirals over possibilities, feels that if he can see fates, he should be able to change fates
- Too scared to check Jackdaw's full fate until he can figure out how to change fate
- Currently travelling along with Jackdaw to find the true chosen one of the [[Magic Items/Skell Necklace\|Skell Necklace]], thriving in the unpredictability of adventuring
- Jackdaw's actual "intense suffering" in the future is a result of Markhor almost dying in an effort to save Jackdaw during the true cataclysm
- His ability to know about future events has led to him taking on more responsibility than he should be forced to handle; his journey is able learning to define the boundaries for himself
