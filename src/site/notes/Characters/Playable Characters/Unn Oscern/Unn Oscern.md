## **Backstory:**
---
- Child of two higher-ups in the [[Factions/Cult of the Lead Swan\|Cult of the Lead Swan]]; born in the [[Locations/Tharin/Anadrie/Dunns/Oscern Manor\|Oscern Manor]] in [[Locations/Tharin/Anadrie/Sevequaris/Sevequaris\|Sevequaris]]
- Raised under AIC ([[Factions/Anti-Icon Coalition\|Anti-Icon Coalition]]) supervision to be turned into a new god and destroy the current pantheon
- Mostly absent parents meant that he didn't receive much attention as a child; only really had [[Characters/Playable Characters/Guilloché Maréchal/Guilloché Maréchal\|Guilloché Maréchal]], his butler 
- The culture of his friend groups and social circle revolved around everyone complaining about their lives and glorifying the hardships they are dealing with
- Since he had no real frame of reference, he felt he had no way to garner sympathy, and so in absence of sympathy, he switched to trying to gain respect
- Tried to succeed his endurance training at all costs, becoming exactly what the cult wanted him to become
- Became obsessive with being ahead of everyone else and seeming invulnerable, only finding solace in Guilloché
- When the time came, he underwent the ritual, and he managed to power through the pain despite how reaming it felt
- However, due to [[Characters/Playable Characters/Bolora Dalai/Bolora Dalai\|Bolora Dalai]] destroying their headquarters and assassinating many of the cult members, Unn no longer had enough of a follower base to truly sustain himself
- Slowly he fades out of existence as his follower base dwindles
- After finding out the cult's plan and realizing Guilloché would not leave to save himself, Unn is actively trying to prevent Kragnyr from being unsealed as his final goal before he ceases to exist
- Escapes with [[Characters/Playable Characters/Rei/Rei\|Rei]], [[Characters/Playable Characters/Ligita/Ligita\|Ligita]], [[Characters/Playable Characters/Fabris/Fabris\|Fabris]], and Guilloché
- DEFINITELY harbors feelings for Guilloché 
- Visual reference to Cernunnos, although nature themes center more on tree blights and fungi
## **Etymology:**
---
> "Unn Oscern" is an anagram of Cernunnos, a largely forgotten god, only existing nowadays based on a few minute appearances in the historic record.
