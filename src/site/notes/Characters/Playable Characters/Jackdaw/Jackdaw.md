## **Backstory:**
---
- Born into the Ripper family, with extraordinary powers and emerald feathers instead of his parents' black feathers
- Lives in [[Locations/Tharin/Muejiza/Galapeak/Galapeak\|Galapeak]], a small city nestled in the mountains just north of [[Locations/Tharin/Muejiza/Muejiza\|Muejiza]]
- In Galapeak, there is the Windliq Prophecy, which states that the [[Magic Items/Skell Necklace\|Skell Necklace]] would pick a chosen one who would save the world in its most dire hour
- When the people saw his unusual colouration, he was expected to be the hero, but Jackdaw just wanted to live a peaceful life
- When he reached adulthood, he was presented with the necklace, only for it to reject him
- He is still asked to keep the necklace until the real chosen one is found, since they believe his colouration to be a lucky charm
- Clings to that rejection like a lifeline, wanting to maintain his own sense of agency
- A part of him does hold a desire for adventure, but he is so caught up in rejecting society that he loops back around to not truly living for himself
- Painted his feathers black to disguise himself and changed his name to Jackdaw to be more "common"
- Meets [[Characters/Playable Characters/Markhor/Markhor\|Markhor]] when he asks to take Jackdaw's feathers for divinations
- Eventually becomes friends with Markhor, a grand diviner, because Markhor saw through his disguise and yet still didn't ask him to be more than he wanted to be
- Gathers materials and ingredients for Markhor's teas and divinations
- When the Inkscourges ravage the surrounding lands and decimate the populations of the nearby towns, they are confident that this is the world-ending disaster
- Jackdaw decides to set out to find the true chosen one
- Eventually, he and Markhor will help resolve the [[Inkscourges\|Inkscourges]] without the prophecy, and the twist will be that the true disaster has not yet come, and will actually be a result of their actions to seal the Inkscourges
- As Jackdaw returns home, the Skell bonds with Jackdaw, signalling the start of the true cataclysm: the escape of [[Characters/The Pantheon/Kragnyr\|Kragnyr]] from his mountain prison
- His journey is about reclaiming what you love when society has made you hate it
## **Etymology:**
---
>"Jackdaw" references the bird of the same name, and his full name is a pun on "Jack the Ripper".
