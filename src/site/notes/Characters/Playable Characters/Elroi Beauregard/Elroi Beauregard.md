## **Backstory:**
---
- Sells glass and mirrors with the [[Factions/Waxing Crescent Guild\|Waxing Crescent Guild]]
- Good friends with [[Characters/Playable Characters/Katherine Avaldi/Katherine Avaldi\|Katherine Avaldi]]
- Rarely shows his face to the public, although his sunny and docile personality makes people more comfortable around him
- During the day, he runs his store, although he stores the mirrors in a separate room because he can't bare to look at his own face
- During the night, he plays ace detective against Katherine, although coincidentally, every time anyone else manages to get close to capturing Katherine, he comes in and forces them out of the operation
- Despite his cheery disposition, he has serious self-loathing and feels shameful for feeling sad due to how good a life he has lived
- Feels that he can't complain, that he must fade into the background, and yet he still craves attention
- Secretly binges on alcohol and food to distract himself, only serving to make him hate himself more
- Long ago, [[Characters/The Pantheon/Bali-Baoli\|Bali-Baoli]] and [[Characters/The Pantheon/Kragnyr\|Kragnyr]] were great friends due to the similarities of their domains (although due to Kragnyr being of a more primitive intelligence, it functioned as slightly more of a owner-pet relationship)
- Kragnyr is a beast made completely of large chunks of volcanic glass; Icon of fire, both in how it creates and destroys, but ultimately in how it consumes and grows
- Over time, as Kragnyr slowly collected more and more fire and grew larger and larger, it began to grow more and more violent and destructive
- To prevent Kragnyr from causing too much damage, Bali-Baoli had to make a decision, and she decided to seal Kragnyr away inside a mountain
- However, in the process, a small piece broke off, and due to how Kragnyr functions, it split into a separate consciousness
- After centuries of burrowing out of the mountain, it manages to free itself
- Attracted to Elroi's store due to his line of work (and also the levels of indulgence it sensed from the shop)
- Finding the beast consuming the heat and glass around his shop, Elroi quickly fell in love with the little guy
- Took it in as a pet and named him Svardog (technically Svarog, but Elroi can't help himself)
- Became very attached to Svardog because it was something who could love him more than he could ever love himself
- Slowly, as Svardog grows larger, his temperature also increases, now uncomfortable to the touch; how long until it starts to actually burn?
- Continued his friendly rivalry with Katherine, his closest friend within the guild
- When Katherine came into his store to ask him for his help in stopping the war to free her genie patron from the Pralucerna, she was naturally distrusting of Svardog as he was very clearly a remnant of an icon
- Still, she allowed Svardog to come along, and now Elroi is travelling alongside Katherine to help her on her journey
- Because of all the trauma and hardships Katherine has been through, he feels that his life has been too good to ever complain about it, and thus he doesn't believe that he can truly be depressed
- Still, days of keeping up his happy facade are beginning to weigh him down, especially as their journey leads them against the Vantegramen-Wydell War.
## **Etymology:**
---
> "Elroi" means "God who sees me" in Hebrew, from אֵל (ʾel) "God" and רָאָה (raʾa) "to see". Beauregard comes from French "beau" meaning "beautiful" and "regard" meaning "look" or "aspect".
