## **Backstory:**
---
- The myth of the first rabbit turning into the moon was false
- Rabbit was not given purpose by turning into the moon; rather became the groundskeeper of the moon, cultivating the silver dusted forests and wilderness of [[Locations/Planes/Afterlives/Jantikata\|Jantikata]] and [[Locations/Planes/Afterlives/Jagtmarker\|Jagtmarker]]
- Took the form of an entirely jade rabbit
- Eventually retired to the depths of the [[Locations/Planes/Lunar Courts\|Lunar Courts]] to oversee the maintenance of the moon and the Lunar Courts
- Told by [[Characters/The Pantheon/Vidatur\|Vidatur]] that they could work as equals
- As Vidatur was forced to take on [[Characters/The Pantheon/Nagaiame\|Nagaiame]]'s role, their friendship gradually fell to the wayside and Rei felt neglected
- Hates how much Vidatur has changed because of their divine system and how miserable he always seems
- Has determined that the system of divinity is too rigid and needs to be restructured
- If she is to go against Vidatur for his own good, she needs to use power not bestowed by Vidatur, and so she turns herself into a halfling as a more dextrous form before completely relinquishing Vidatur's magic
- Parts of her are still completely made of jade, some green, some white
- Was the person Beo sought advice from when he was deciding whether or not to disobey the courts; she obviously encouraged him
- Joined the AIC ([[Factions/Anti-Icon Coalition\|Anti-Icon Coalition]]), a group located in [[Locations/Tharin/Anadrie/Dunns/Dunns\|Dunns]] dedicated to the destruction and restructure of the pantheon and partners with the [[Factions/Cult of the Lead Swan\|Cult of the Lead Swan]], to find like-minded individuals who want to destroy the pantheon
- Because of her previous direct experience with the Icons, she is regarded as a highly valuable member and is brought into the "[[Characters/Playable Characters/Unn Oscern/Unn Oscern\|Unn]] Project", a project to turn a boy named Unn into a god so he can wreak havoc on the pantheon
- After learning about the cult's plan to unseal [[Characters/The Pantheon/Kragnyr\|Kragnyr]], Rei is immediately aghast, and when [[Characters/Playable Characters/Bolora Dalai/Bolora Dalai\|Bolora Dalai]] destroys the cult headquarters, Rei escapes the AIC in the chaos along with [[Characters/Playable Characters/Ligita/Ligita\|Ligita]], [[Characters/Playable Characters/Fabris/Fabris\|Fabris]], Unn, and [[Characters/Playable Characters/Guilloché Maréchal/Guilloché Maréchal\|Guilloché]] they are trying to put a stop to the Cult of the Lead Swan's plan to unleash Kragnyr
## **Etymology:**
