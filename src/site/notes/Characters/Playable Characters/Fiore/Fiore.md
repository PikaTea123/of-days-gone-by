## **Backstory:**
---
- [[Characters/The Pantheon/Ergehen\|Ergehen]] helps [[Characters/The Pantheon/Bali-Baoli\|Bali-Baoli]] in sealing away [[Characters/The Pantheon/Kragnyr\|Kragnyr]] beneath the [[Locations/Tharin/Muejiza/Glassen Slade\|Glassen Slade]] because she wants to take his domain later; pieces of Kragnyr break off
	- one turns into [[Magic Items/Nagaiame’s Gifts/Living Immolation\|Living Immolation]]
	- one turns into [[Characters/Playable Characters/Elroi Beauregard/Elroi Beauregard\|Svardog]]
	- Ergehen turns one shard into Fiore as a sort of symbol of her victory over Kragnyr; casts Fiore into the [[Locations/Tharin/Marzol/Lapisean Ocean/Lapisean Ocean\|Lapisean Ocean]] afterwards
- The intense heat of Kragnyr's shard is rapidly cooled by the water and causes microfractures to form in the glass, although she is able to rapidly heal it just as fast
- Eventually, Bali-Baoli takes pity on Fiore and whisks her to shore, leaving her on the steps of the [[Locations/Tharin/Marzol/King's Light Fencing Studio\|King's Light Fencing Studio]] which actively worshipped Bali-Baoli
- Fiore is obsessed with finding the meaning of her creation, even if it exhausts her
- Every moment spent on worrying about one's own problems means less time spent on fixing the world's problems
- She loathes Ergehen for bringing her into the world and denying her the ability to rest, but still can't help but continue trying to solve the world's issues
- Has decided that the current pantheon is too rife with discourse and disfunction to properly support the world, and that it needs to be completely restructured
- Joined the AIC ([[Factions/Anti-Icon Coalition\|Anti-Icon Coalition]]), a group located in [[Locations/Tharin/Anadrie/Dunns/Dunns\|Dunns]] dedicated to the destruction and restructure of the pantheon and partnered with the [[Factions/Cult of the Lead Swan\|Cult of the Lead Swan]], to find like-minded individuals who want to restructure the pantheon
- Because she is directly infused with the power of an Icon, she is regarded as a highly valuable member and is brought into the "[[Characters/Playable Characters/Unn Oscern/Unn Oscern\|Unn]] Project", a project to turn a boy named Unn into a god so he can wreak havoc on the pantheon
- After learning about the cult's plan to unseal Kragnyr, Fiore wants out, and when [[Characters/Playable Characters/Bolora Dalai/Bolora Dalai\|Bolora Dalai]] destroys the cult headquarters, Fiore escapes the AIC along with [[Characters/Playable Characters/Ligita/Ligita\|Ligita]], [[Characters/Playable Characters/Rei/Rei\|Rei]], Unn, and [[Characters/Playable Characters/Guilloché Maréchal/Guilloché Maréchal\|Guilloché]]; now they are trying to put a stop to the Cult of the Lead Swan's plan to unleash Kragnyr
- Design is a mixture between fencing garb and deep sea diving gear, emphasizing the helmet with a fusion of the fencing net and the classic crossed bars across the face of the diving helmet; an emoticon-like face projection glows behind the bars
- By healing the microfractures into specific patterns and passing Ergehen's light through the shard of Kragnyr in different ways, Fiore is able to control her expression through shaping the refracted light
## **Etymology:**
---
> "Fiore" comes from Italian, meaning "flower" in Italian; also a reference to Fiore dei Liberi, one of the great fencing masters.
