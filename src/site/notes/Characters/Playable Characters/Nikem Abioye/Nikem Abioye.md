## **Backstory:**
---
- An ambassador for [[Locations/Tharin/Vantegramen\|Vantegramen]]; aunt is Ifuna, a knight and operating as a bodyguard for the mission
- Wydell never received the notice of an ambassador coming, causing the guards of [[Locations/Tharin/Wydell/Wydell\|Wydell]] to attack Nikem as if she was an invader
- Ifuna stepped forth to protect her while Nikem hides, Ifuna gets injured while fighting off the guards
- Just before Ifuna is killed, Nikem runs in with [[Magic Items/Runenbijl\|Runenbijl]], seals Ifuna inside to save her
- Ends up releasing the ancient evil that was sealed inside, unleasing chaos upon [[Locations/Tharin/Wydell/Wydell Castle\|Wydell Castle]]
- Spent the last seven years trying to figure out how to free her aunt and almost gave up, but a new lead on the creator of the axe has sent her back out to find an answer
- Ifuna is now trapped as the carvings on the axe head; "speaks" to Nikem by shifting as the carvings to form pictograms
- Nikem feels both responsible for Ifunanya and also feels she has to reseal away the evil
## **Etymology:**
---
>Originally was Nkem Abioye, where "Nkem" was short for "Nkemdilim" which means "that which is mine belongs to me" in Igbo. "Abioye" means "born into royalty" in Yoruba. "Ifuna" comes from "Ifunanya", which translates to "love", but also to "to see in one's eye" as she can only communicate by being seen.
