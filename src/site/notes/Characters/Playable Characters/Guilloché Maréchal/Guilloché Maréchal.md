## **Backstory:**
---
- Butler hired at a very young age and practically raised by the family he was hired by in the [[Locations/Tharin/Anadrie/Dunns/Oscern Manor\|Oscern Manor]]
- Beat down every time he made a mistake; very critical of self; obsessive over the idea of duty
- Employers were members of the [[Factions/Cult of the Lead Swan\|Cult of the Lead Swan]] who wanted him to take care of their son, [[Characters/Playable Characters/Unn Oscern/Unn Oscern\|Unn Oscern]], while they were off doing cult duties
- The cult was working with the AIC ([[Factions/Anti-Icon Coalition\|Anti-Icon Coalition]]) to unseal Kragnyr while the pantheon was already in disorder from [[Characters/The Pantheon/Nagaiame\|Nagaiame]]'s death, then also releasing Unn as a new god to restructure the pantheon
- Has a pocket watch that ticks down until the plan is set to start, in which he will certainly die due to how close Kragnyr is to his location; his canine ears mean he is constantly hearing the ticking, which he obsesses over, driving him crazy
- To keep himself sane, he wears a muzzle stuffed with star anise inside to keep himself doped up, and when he removes it is when his true powers are unleashed
- Believes he should have made peace with his inevitable death by now to better accomplish his duty to Unn
- Unn has tried to get him to leave and save himself, but Guilloché refuses, believing himself to be undeserving of being saved
- As Unn begins to fade from existence, Guilloché dedicates himself to preventing Unn's death; harbors some feelings for Unn
- An Ethiopian myth states that the Jackal was originally barred from entering Noah's Ark, since Noah thought them unworthy of being saved until god commanded him to let them on
- Anubis, often said to have a Jackal head, also is a god of judgement--just over the dead
- Design is an homage to French butler/maître d'hôtel tropes
- Muzzle and bloodshot eyes visually elicit rabies
- Dogs herding livestock (Unn)
- Escapement is both a pocket watch term and alluding to escapism, how he dopes himself to distract from his own mortality; syncopal is a reference to syncope meaning fainting, but also evokes syncopation in reference to the escapement and also syncope as in omission, as he hides all his needs
- Star anise can induce seizures in too high amounts; Guilloché is likely nearing some limit
## **Etymology:**
---
>"Guilloché" is a a decorative technique in which a very precise, intricate, and repetitive pattern is mechanically engraved into an underlying material via engine turning, often used on pocket watches. Maréchal is a reference to Marshall Rosenberg, the creator of NVC in which the Jackal is used as a metaphor for self judgement and criticism.
