## **Backstory:**
---
- Jetai and Àtoi were tasked with destroying a group of beast ravaging the wilds of [[Locations/Tharin/Wydell/Wydell\|Wydell]]
- These beasts turned out to be a small group of [[Inkscourges\|Inkscourges]] that were released during one of the escape attempts of a member of [[Characters/The Pantheon/Nagaiame\|Nagaiame]]'s kobold guards
- After realizing their regular weapons and spells were having little effect against the Inkscourges (due to the Inkscourges being of quasi-divine strength), they decided to use a last resort
- Leading the Inkscourges to an ancient trapped tomb, Àtoi volunteered to use the shifting layout of the tomb to hold the Inkscourges in long enough for Jetai to set up the explosive in the tomb and for them to both escape and detonate it
- They agreed on having ten minutes to perform the operation, and if either of them weren't out by that time, the other would detonate the explosive
- Only Jetai made it out in time and detonated the bomb, despite the pain it caused her
- Àtoi in the tomb was caught off guard by a layout shift and was overwhelmed by the Inkscourges
- As the Inkscourges were separating his soul from his body, Whisp's mother detonated it, temporarily dissipating the Inkscourges and interrupting the soul rending process, fusing the husband's soul with the Inkscourges
- This fusion wanders the ruins of the tomb for the next few months
- Jetai then gives birth to [[Characters/Playable Characters/Whisp/Whisp\|Whisp]]'s egg, although due to her and her husband's difference in species, she assumed it would be inviable like all the ones before it, although unbeknownst to her, it was miraculously viable
- Unable to handle losing both Àtoi and another newborn, she floated the egg down a river (later to be found by Helle after having hatched)
- Returning to the tomb ruins after a few months to attempt to make peace with Àtoi's death, she encounters the fusion
- At first she was terrified, but after realizing the fusion was not hostile, she managed to tame it with some magic
- The Inkscourge does not seem to recognize itself as partially her husband, but cooperates with her and lives in the charred skull of Àtoi that managed to survive the explosion
- The remaining divine power of the Inkscourges have allowed her to make a pact with the fusion
- It has given her hope that one day she will find a way to reseparate Àtoi's soul or at least bring back his memories, even though unbeknownst to her, that is impossible
- She now recharts all the areas they've adventured to and all the spots they've made memories in, using her newfound powers to protect them, holding on to the idea that she can save her husband
## **Etymology:**
---
>"Jetai" is a butchering of the French "j'étais" meaning "I was", and "Àtoi" is a truncated version of "à toi" meaning "to you" or "yours".
