## **Backstory:**
---
- Barbaro is raised in [[Locations/Planes/Feywild/Chiron Racecourse\|Chiron Racecourse]], home to a centaur racing ring in the [[Locations/Planes/Feywild/Feywild\|Feywild]], [[Characters/The Pantheon/Scaenatio\|Scaenatio]]'s homeplane and Land of the Fey, where people can bet on the centaurs like racehorses
- Value was only placed on the medalists and the fastest centaurs; the others were treated as nothing more than animals
- He was raised up and then emotionally abandoned over and over again as his placement fluctuated throughout the years
- Barbaro runs into [[Characters/Side Characters/Riklig\|Riklig]], an Eladrin boy, at one of the events and they become close friends
- Riklig often steals clothes from the townspeople and brings them to Barbaro to unravel them and then remake them a few sizes smaller to prank the townspeople
- The winters of the Feywild begin getting harsher and harsher in the years leading up to [[Characters/Side Characters/Vetle\|Vetle]]'s suicide, bringing snow for the first times to the Feywild
- Riklig often sneaks Barbaro out to play in the mountains
- One day, they witness an avalanche barreling towards the horse racetrack where everyone from the executives to the racehorses themselves are currently gathered to enjoy the festivities as the horse races end for the early winter
- Riklig hops on Barbaro and they race down the mountain, thankfully fast enough to outrun the avalanche and warn the people in Chiron Racecourse
- Only, Barbaro stops midway
- Barbaro reasons that this would be the perfect opportunity to end the centaur racing ring and the cycle of suffering it represents
- The centaurs were never going to be allowed lives anyways; there wasn't even a sense of community between the centaurs given how oppressed they were. It would be best to end it now, even knowing that it would cause mass death
- Riklig can't believe Barbaro's train of thought and they argue, but Barbaro dashes away from Chiron Racecourse, leaving them all to die
- In a fit of rage and grief, Riklig runs into the woods, and after a few minutes in silence, Barbaro goes searching for him
- Barbaro finds Riklig two hours later, Riklig still wet after having recently dragged himself from a frozen lake just 30 minutes prior
- Barbaro watches in horror as Riklig fearfully freezes to death
- Vetle, who happened to be nearby after being called to the Feywild by Scaenatio to fix the harsh winters, finds the freezing Riklig and decides to take his soul to the [[Locations/Planes/Lunar Courts\|Lunar Courts]] himself, trying to ease the passing of the child with gentle words
- Barbaro, after witnessing the horror of slow death, is wracked with guilt for leaving all those people to die and runs off
- Stumbling through the woods, he ends up falling from a steep outcropping and shattering one of his hind legs
- During his training in Chiron's racetrack, he was taught that a fractured leg meant certain euthanization; he uses Rimegill Mushrooms as painkillers to continue on
- He overdoses on the shrooms which he was using as a painkiller to flee; they create vivid hallucinations, and as a trauma response, Barbaro accepts them as true and begins building an entire narrative around those hallucinations, believing that Vetle will be his saviour and that Vetle has already saved Riklig
- He hears "Riklig" calling out to him in his state of mushroom-induced hallucinatory psychosis, leading him through the woods
- He ends up finding a portal out of the Feywild that drops him just outside of the town of Dunns, where he is taken to the hospital
- During his hallucinations and traumatic panic, his brain has manufactured:

1.  Vetle was very kind and took Barbaro and Riklig back to his domain to save them
2.  Vetle trained the two of them for years
3.  Vetle told them they were finally ready to go back to the Feywild
4.  Riklig didn't want to go back, so Barbaro had to knock him out and carry him back to the portal
5.  When they returned, Barbaro's captors from Chiron found them and Barbaro and Riklig were separated in the chase
6.  Barbaro breaks his leg while running but manages to drag himself to another portal, which took him to Dunns

- None of the numbered events really happened, but Barbaro believes that series of events to be true
- Now that his leg has fully healed, he is setting off to find either Vetle or Riklig, although he will surely be disappointed to find that both of them are now dead
- Perhaps on this journey, he will also rediscover the truths behind his past, reopening old wounds of abandonment, accepting past sins, and learning how to continue on without forgiveness
## **Etymology:**
---
> "Barbaro" is an allusion to the winner of the 2006 Kentucky Derby who shattered his legs and had to be euthanized.
