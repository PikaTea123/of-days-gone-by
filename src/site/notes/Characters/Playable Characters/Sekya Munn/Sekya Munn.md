## **Backstory:**
---
- Is a writer, obsessed with fantasy and heroics
- Once had a role model mage ([[Characters/Side Characters/Helle Agnes\|Helle Agnes]]) that she vowed to best one day
- Helle Agnes died to an enemy group that she spared
- Grew up wondering why role model didn’t just kill them
- Joined the Benevol Academy of Magedom
- Actually became part of an adventuring guild to gain some inspiration for her book
- Book kept getting rejected because it was "unrealistic" and "asinine" with its happy ending
- First mission was to deliver ambassador notice from [[Locations/Tharin/Vantegramen\|Vantegramen]] to [[Locations/Tharin/Wydell/Wydell\|Wydell]]
- Adventuring party ends up being ambushed on first mission, all but Wizard and enemy leader die
- Wizard has opportunity to end enemy leader, kills her to avoid being like role model
- Goal of journey is to notify all her party's families and the enemy's families of the death of their loved ones
- Fighting to keep the happy ending of her book, wants to die in her delusion than believe that there is truly no good option in the world
## **Etymology:**
---
>Originally Suk-yeong Mun, with both Korean *suk* (淑) and *yeong* (英) come from hanja that mean "hero", and *mun* (文) comes from the character meaning "writing". Altered to fit linguistic feel of Benevol better.
