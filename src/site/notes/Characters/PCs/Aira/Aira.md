## **Backstory:**
---
- Was formerly a military supply manager/cook, dreamt of being an opera singer
- Had one true friend that was a renowned mage soldier ([[Characters/Side Characters/Helle Agnes\|Helle Agnes]]) in the [[Locations/Tharin/Vantegramen\|Vantegramen]] army, she went off to battle and never returned
- Aira took Helle's appearance to make sure she never forgot her
- Now runs the tavern in the neutral town just beyond the warzone
- Always pretending to be what people want her to be
- Put on so many masks that she's unsure of who she truly is
- Decides to pack up and adventure with [[Characters/PCs/Pseudaesthesia/Pseudaesthesia\|Pseudaesthesia]] because none of her friendships feel real
- Pseudaesthesia encouraged her to pursue her singing, Aira became a bard
- Wants to complain, too focused on comforting Pseudaesthesia so she bottles up her own pains
- Afraid of losing another friend
- Now trying to find a way to avenge Helle while dealing with the chaos that came with taking Helle's identity
## **Etymology:**
---
> "Aira" comes from Finnish "airut" meaning messenger, referencing how she feels eternally shoved into a supporting role. It's also "aria" scrambled.
