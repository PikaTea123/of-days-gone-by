## **Backstory:**
---
- Got lost in the Feywild as a child
- Raised by [[Characters/The Pantheon/Scaenatio\|Scaenatio]], although the identity of her caretaker was unknown to her
- Only acknowledged when she had a problem, and growing up, was only given attention through pity and "having it worse than others"
- Became a wandering artist preaching the values of her Icon
- Chose painting over acting to get her icon to hate her, to victimize herself
- Decided to seek inspiration through storm-chasing, ended up running straight into the warzone of [[Locations/Tharin/Vantegramen\|Vantegramen]]
- Encountered [[Characters/PCs/Aira/Aira\|Aira]] who followed her into the warzone, where they grow close and end up getting involved in the war
- Currently trying to break herself down enough, make her story tragic enough, to gain the love of her Icon
- Icon keeps pushing her further and further by withholding affection

## **Etymology:**
---
>"Pseudaesthesia" is a word meaning "phantom pain", referring to how she tries to artificially make herself more miserable.
