## **Backstory:**
---
- Original commander of the [[Factions/Cult of the Lead Swan\|Cult of the Lead Swan]], seduced by the idea of power to avoid being a burden
- Was in a relationship with her second in command
- Partner started laying the guilt on thick for Nimavaros dragging him into the operation and making him risk his life
- One day, her partner faked his death in one of their operations, sending Nimavaros into a spiral where she ran from her position as cult leader and barricaded herself in a labyrinth
- In the labyrinth, she spent the next 100 years hiding away, surviving off the barest resources and befriending the few animals inside
- Eventually, [[Characters/PCs/Petla Erneut/Petla Erneut\|Petla Erneut]] came to raid the labyrinth, and after a brief scuffle, they befriended each other
- After learning of her partner's betrayal, she vowed to take her revenge
## **Etymology:**
---
>"Nímaváros" (νήμαβάρος) consists of "Níma", which in Greek means "thread", and "Város", which means "burden".
