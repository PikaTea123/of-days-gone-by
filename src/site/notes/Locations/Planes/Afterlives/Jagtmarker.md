- [[Characters/The Pantheon/Vidatur\|Vidatur]]'s afterlife, although originally belonged to [[Characters/The Pantheon/Nagaiame\|Nagaiame]] when he was still alive
- Was originally a realm of barrenness and deprivation, but after [[Locations/Planes/Afterlives/Cinders\|Cinders]] weakened due to [[Characters/The Pantheon/Kragnyr\|Kragnyr]]'s sealing and completely died out after Nagaiame ceased to be maintaining it once he died, Vidatur changed Jagtmarker to avoid overlap
- With [[Characters/Playable Characters/Rei/Rei\|Rei]]'s help, they revitalized the realm, turning it into an eternal hunting ground, where the souls are hunted by Vidatur only to rematerialize and repeat the cycle until their complete disintegration, often as a punishment for being malevolently predatory in some way during their life.
