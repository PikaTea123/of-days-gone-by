- [[Characters/The Pantheon/Scaenatio\|Scaenatio]]'s afterlife
- A plane of celebration for those who lived interesting enough lives to catch Scaenatio's attention
- Whether she made their life a comedy or tragedy, this plane serves as a final bow and wrap up party to those who played on her "stage"
- Often screens moments of others' lives during the celebration as entertainment
