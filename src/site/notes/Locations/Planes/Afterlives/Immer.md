- [[Characters/The Pantheon/Ergehen\|Ergehen]]'s afterlife
- Ergehen is the Icon of endless chasing; she grieves those who died before they were able to achieve what they sought
- Her afterlife uses mirrors, light, and illusion to give these souls a "second chance" at achieving their goals
- She completely replicates the scenario of the person's death and let them continue from there
- Most of the time, these souls do not even realize they've died until they begin to disintegrate
