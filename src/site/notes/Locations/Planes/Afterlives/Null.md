- [[Characters/The Pantheon/Sjörnur\|Sjörnur]]'s "afterlife"
- Sjörnur doesn't agree with the idea of holding souls past their time and instead disintegrates any souls that are sent his way to hasten them to their final rest
- Null is the ocean of aether in which he disintegrates the souls
- [[Characters/The Pantheon/Ard'okaal\|Ard'okaal]] also sends her souls here as she doesn't care about the souls after they are destroyed and would rather fast track the process
